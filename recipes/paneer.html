Butter Paneer Masala

Description

One of the best Indian dishes in the word, so are you feeling hungry? Alright, so let's deep dive into the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Red and juicy tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Raw Cashews
  • Butter
  • Paneer
  • Spices and Herbs

Steps

  1. Pour 1 tablespoon oil to a hot pan
  2. Add onions and tomatoes
  3. Now add spices and herbs
  4. Blend it and prepare gravy
  5. Add Paneer
  6. Stir it well
