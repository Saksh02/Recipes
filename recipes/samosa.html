Samosaa

Description

One of the most common street foods in India, almost every indian had an experience with samosa.

But for now, feeling hungry after seeing samosa, let's understand the recipe first.

Ingredients

  • Boiled Potato
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Cumin
  • Ginger, peas and cilantro
  • Flour

Steps

  1. First, Boil potatoes
  2. Then, make a dough of flour
  3. Add the spice powders
  4. Make a cone shape with dough
  5. Now, potato filling
  6. Fry it in oil
