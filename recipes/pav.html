Pav Bhaji

Description

Nobody in this entire world can have such joy, eating pav bhaji while you are hungry.

Great! Let's cook and eat, but firstly, we need to know the recipe

Ingredients

  • Pav
  • Vegetables
  • Butter - a lot!
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat the oil
  2. Blend Vegetables
  3. Cook vegetables on medium flame
  4. Prepare the gravy
  5. Fry pav with butter
  6. Serve and eat
