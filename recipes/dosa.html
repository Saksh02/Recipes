Masala Dosa

Description

One of the tastiest snacks to have, while you are hungry.

So, feeling hungry, let's begin with the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Dosa Batter
  • Spices
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Pulses
  • Oil
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Prepare and make the batter
  2. Blend the batter with rice
  3. Ferment the batter
  4. Boil Potatoes
  5. Fry the potatoes with spices
  6. Fill potatoes with masala dosa and fry
